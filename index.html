About Me

My name is Isaac Swartzer, I am 21 years old, and I am from Minnetonka, Minnesota. I am a student in the U of M Coding Bootcamp. Also, I am a Junior at the University of Minnesota - Twin Cities where I study Computer Science in the College of Liberal Arts. I decided to study Comp Sci and enrolled into the bootcamp because I took an Introduction to Programming Class at the University and I fell in love with the madness that is coding.
My hobbies include rock climbing, photography and eating. I climb at the Minneapolis Bouldering Project and I am able to project v6~ routes. I am not a professional photographer by any means but I enjoy finding beauty in the everyday nuances that often go unnoticed. Also, I love to eat food and try different cuisines from different cultures because it is one of the few mediums that any human can relate too, no matter your ethnicity.
